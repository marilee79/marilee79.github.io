That Famous Preston Night Rodeo

RODEO DAYS PARADE

Thursday, Friday & Saturday

The Rodeo Days Parade is each night, Thursday, Friday and Saturday at 6:30p.m. Line up is at the 2nd North and 1st West. Thursday, Friday and Saturday's parade are the traditional "bands and floats" parades.

"CITY OF FUN" CARNIVAL

Thursday, Friday & Saturday

Following the Rodeo Days Parade. A Ferris wheel, merry-go-round, and more!! Known as one of the best carnivals around. Ride your favorite rides and munch on pronto pups and cotton candy.

RODEO DAYS SIDEWALK SALE

Thursday, Friday & Saturday

Along with our three nights of rodeo, we have parades and a great downtown sidewalk sale. The sidewalk sale brings vendors from our local vicinity and from surrounding states. Everything from jewelry to camping gear to clothes, gifts, signs, decorating items...you name it, you can find it, along with all kinds of great food booths. Delicious foods, drinks, sweets can be found as you stroll down Preston's main street and Oneida Street.
