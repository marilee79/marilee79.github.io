Site Name

Southeast Idaho's Trifecta

Preston | Soda Springs | Fish Haven

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is a resource for those who live in and around Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven, Idaho. This website will have all sorts of things that are going on in the community. There will be weather information, articles, and events that are happening in the surrounding areas. Visitors will be able to use this site to find places to see and things to do while visiting.
