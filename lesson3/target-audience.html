Target Audience

The audience for this website will range in ages of 4-104. They will be both male and female. They will be residents of Preston, Soda Springs, or Fish Haven, Idaho. Others who may be visiting the area will find this website to useful. The users will be in all income levels. Their similar interests will be in the many things you can do outdoors; hiking, biking, horse back riding, boating, conoeing, paddleboarding, and picnicing.

Personas

Jackson Davis

Jackson is a 48 year old Vice President of an internet security company. His yearly income is roughly $500,000. Jackson loves to get away from the hustle and bustle of a large city. His family comes to Preston, Idaho once a year to spend time on a ranch for a week. Jackson is married and has 3 children, two boys and one girl. They love to ride horses, drive cattle and feed the chicken. Jackson's family also enjoys canoeing and paddleboarding at the nearby reservoirs. The Davis family enjoys exploring the many different hiking trails near the ranch.

Kelly Smith

Lives in Preston, Idaho. She is an Event Planner for an events center in the middle of town. Her salary is $50,000/year. She is the mother of a two year boy. She is 25 years old and has been married for 5 years. Kelly has been a planner for 3 years and plans many different types of events: weddings, receptions, galas, birthday parties, and christmas parties.
